Classic Pancake Recipe

Instructions

Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.

In another bowl, whisk the egg, milk, and melted butter.

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and whisk until just combined.

Heat a lightly oiled griddle and pour batter to form pancakes.

Cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook until golden brown.
